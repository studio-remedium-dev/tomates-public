Privacy Policy for Tomates

About Tomates

Tomates is a Pomodoro application developed by Studio RemediumDev, a division of 9434-5782 Quebec Inc., designed to help you manage your time more effectively. Our application allows users to set work and break intervals to maximize productivity. Tomates is built as an offline-first application, ensuring your data privacy and security by design.

Privacy First Policy

Tomates is committed to ensuring your privacy. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information or usage data. The application operates offline without the need for internet connectivity, and no data leaves your device.

No Data Collection

Our application does not collect any form of personal information from its users. As an offline-first app, Tomates ensures that all your activity and data remain on your device without transmitting them to any servers or third-party services.

No Cookies or Tracking

Tomates does not use cookies or any form of tracking mechanisms. Your use of the application is private, secure, and free from any form of online tracking or analytics.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
